Clean Fuel Checker

This web application explores the proportion population per country with primary reliance on clean fuel and technologies for cooking (in millions)

According to the WHO, Clean fuels and technologies are defined in accordance with WHO guidelines for indoor air quality: household fuel combustion. This includes households primarily relying on electricity, biogas, natural gas, liquified petroleum gas (LPG), solar or alcohol fuels for cooking.
